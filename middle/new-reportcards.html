To the Parent or Guardian:

This report gives you information about the progress your student is making in school, including attendance, grades and the grade point average (GPA) for middle school students. (Grade 6-8). Issued each nine weeks during the school year, this report is your copy to keep for your records. Each part of the report is described below. If you have questions about any information on the report, if you find errors in the information, or if you need additional information about your student's educational progress, please contact your student's guidance counselor. Middle School Cumulative GPA: Comprehensive grade point average of all courses taken in middle school and posted to permanent record.

Middle School YTD GPA: Current school year grade point
average to date.

SCHOOL - This is the school in which your student is enrolled.

SEMESTER - The 180-day school year is divided into two semesters. The first semester ends in December; the second ends the school year. Some subjects or courses last one semester and may be taken either semester. Other subjects or courses last two semesters or the full school year.

GRADE PERIOD - The school year is divided into four grade reporting periods.




 

GRADING KEY - This key defines each grade and gives the numerical range (0-100) for each letter grade.

PERIOD (P) - The class period each day the student is assigned to the subject or course is identified in this column.

SUBJECT - The subject or course name.

TEACHER- The name of the teacher is identified for the course or subject.

GRADING PERIOD - The numbered columns provide space for reporting grades for each grading period. The two columns labeled "Sem" report the semester average.

YEAR- This is the current academic year.

GRADE- This is the student's current assigned grade.

ABSENCES (E, U) - These two columns identify the number of absences (excused or unexcused) for the current grading period (nine weeks) by subject.

TARDIES (E, U) - Identifies the number of tardies has completed the 4 Year Plan requirement. (excused or unexcused) for the current grading period by subject.

COMMENTS- These are coded comments (see codes at bottom of front side) for teachers to use in providing additional information about the student's performance in class.

SUBJECT AREA REQUIREMENTS - This displays subjects/credits required for promotion from 8th grade to 9th grade per A++ State of Florida legislation. This displays how many credits your student has earned (to date) in each subject, how many are needed for promotion and how many are currently in-progress.

FINAL - The final grade as posted on the Permanent Record.
